How to open the app:
  1) go to the site "https://movie-recommendation-project-5fkyacthe3qlgrvk3a4gid.streamlit.app/"
  2) if the app is sleeping click in the middle of the screen on "wake up this app" and wait for it to start
  3) youre in!

While in the app:
  1) choose a movie you liked
  2) click "get recommendations"
  3) adjust the settings in the menu in the left
  4) thats it!
